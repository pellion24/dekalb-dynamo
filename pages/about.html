About Us!

We are a grassroots soccer team based out of the North DeKalb Area!

We had the goal of finding a space for us and our friends to play competitive and fast-paced soccer during the summer. After some recruiting and hard-work, we started our first ever competitive season with the ADASL, and had our first match on June 1st, 2025.

Our vision is to grow DeKalb Dynamo from just a men's Sunday-League team into a proper grassroots club, with teams in multiple leagues within the Metro Atlanta soccer scene.

If you like what you're hearing and would like to help out, check out our sponsors tab!
